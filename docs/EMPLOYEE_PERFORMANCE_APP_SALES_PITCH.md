CacheTask is a workforce performance platform that gives companies complete visibility into how their teams work — every task assigned, every hour logged, every day accounted for.

It replaces the disconnected mix of spreadsheets, punch cards, chat threads, and guesswork that most companies still rely on to manage their people. Instead, leadership gets a single dashboard where attendance, task progress, leave balances, and performance metrics are always current and always accurate.

**Who is it for?**

- Startups scaling past 20 employees who need structure without bureaucracy
- Mid-sized companies (50–500 employees) struggling with visibility across teams

- Any organization where managers spend more time chasing updates than making decisions

**What does it solve?**

It eliminates the gap between what leadership *thinks* is happening and what *actually* is. When every task has an owner, every check-in is verified, and every metric is tracked automatically, companies stop losing money to inefficiency and start making decisions based on facts.

## 2. The Problem Companies Face

### No Visibility Into Daily Output

Most managers cannot answer a simple question: *"What did my team accomplish today?"* They rely on end-of-day messages, weekly standups, or quarterly reviews — all of which are delayed, incomplete, and filtered through self-reporting bias.

### Manual Attendance Is Broken

Paper registers, basic punch machines, and honor-system check-ins are easy to manipulate. Buddy punching, late arrivals logged as on-time, and ghost attendance cost companies real money every pay cycle.

### Fake Attendance Is a Real Cost

Without location verification and identity confirmation at the point of check-in, companies have no way to confirm that the person marking attendance is actually present at the workplace. For field teams and remote workers, this problem multiplies.

### Leave Tracking Falls Apart at Scale

When leave balances live in spreadsheets maintained by HR, discrepancies are inevitable. Employees dispute balances. Managers approve leave without checking team coverage. HR spends hours reconciling records that should be automatic.

### No Task Accountability

Tasks assigned over email or chat disappear into inboxes. There is no structured record of what was assigned, to whom, when it was due, and whether it was completed with evidence. Disputes during performance reviews become unavoidable.

### Delayed Insights

By the time leadership sees productivity data — if they see it at all — the quarter is over. Decisions about resource allocation, promotions, and team restructuring are made on outdated information.

### Management Overhead

Managers spend a disproportionate amount of their time collecting status updates, verifying attendance, calculating leave, and compiling reports. This is time that should be spent on strategy, coaching, and decision-making.

## 3. Our Solution

CacheTask addresses each of these problems through a unified, mobile-first platform that works for office, remote, and field employees alike.

### Centralized Performance Dashboard

One screen shows leadership exactly where things stand — tasks completed, attendance rates, leave balances, and team performance scores. No more switching between five different tools to get a complete picture.

### Verified Attendance

Every check-in requires two things: a face verification step and a location confirmation. This eliminates buddy punching, remote fraud, and unverified attendance entries. Every record includes a timestamp, location, and identity confirmation.

### Structured Task Management

Tasks are created, assigned, tracked, and closed within the platform. Each task has a clear owner, a due date, a status, and — when completed — attached evidence of completion. Managers see progress in real time, not days later.

### Real-Time Task Visibility

Kanban boards, timeline views, and status dashboards give managers instant clarity on what is in progress, what is blocked, and what is overdue. No more chasing people for updates.

### Automated Leave Management

Leave balances are calculated automatically. Employees apply through the platform. Managers approve with full visibility into team availability. HR no longer reconciles spreadsheets — the system is the single source of truth.

### Performance Transparency

Every employee can see their own metrics — tasks completed, attendance record, skills tracked, and performance scores. This eliminates the "black box" feeling that erodes trust during review cycles. Managers evaluate based on data, not memory.

## 4. Key Business Benefits

### Increased Productivity

When employees know their output is visible and measured, productivity increases. When managers can identify bottlenecks in real time, they resolve them faster. The result is more work completed per team, per week.

### Reduced Management Overhead

Managers stop spending hours collecting updates, verifying attendance, and compiling reports. The platform does this automatically. Management time is redirected to coaching, strategy, and high-value decision-making.

### Transparent Accountability

Every task has a paper trail. Every attendance record is verified. Every leave request is documented. This eliminates disputes, reduces favoritism perceptions, and creates a culture of fair, evidence-based evaluation.

### Reduced Payroll Leakage

Verified attendance means companies only pay for hours actually worked. Elimination of buddy punching, ghost attendance, and unverified check-ins directly reduces payroll waste.

### Fraud Prevention

Face verification and location checks make it practically impossible to fake attendance. For companies with field teams, delivery staff, or remote workers, this alone can justify the investment.

### Faster Decision Making

Real-time dashboards mean leadership does not wait for monthly reports to understand team performance. Resource allocation, hiring decisions, and project adjustments happen based on current data.

### Improved Employee Engagement

When employees see how they are evaluated and have clear visibility into their own performance, engagement improves. Transparency builds trust. Trust reduces attrition.
